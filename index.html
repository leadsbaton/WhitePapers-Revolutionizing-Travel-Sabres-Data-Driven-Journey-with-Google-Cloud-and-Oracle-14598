Revolutionizing Travel: Sabre’s Data-Driven Journey with Google Cloud and Oracle

Discover how the Oracle and Google Cloud partnership is reshaping the cloud landscape, enabling businesses to seamlessly integrate Oracle and Google Cloud services while leveraging the strengths of each platform. Andi Gutmans, VP & GM, Databases at Google Cloud, will discuss the benefits of migrating Oracle-based applications to Google Cloud, modernizing existing infrastructure, and integrating with Google Cloud's cutting-edge services in AI, analytics, and more.

Dominic Briggs, SVP of Site Reliability Engineering at Sabre, will then join Andi for a fireside chat to share Sabre's experience in leveraging Google Cloud for serverless computing, data analytics, and sustainability initiatives. Discover how Sabre uses Cloud Functions, Cloud Run, and BigQuery to optimize costs, improve scalability, gain data insights, and build a more sustainable future.
